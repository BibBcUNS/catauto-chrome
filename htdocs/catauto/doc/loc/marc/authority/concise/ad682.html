FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Explanation for the deletion of an established heading or subdivision record from an authority file. The replacement heading(s) may be contained in subfield(s) $a.

Appropriate only when Leader/05 (Record status) contains code d (Deleted).

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

  • Both indicator positions are undefined; each contains a blank (#).

■ SUBFIELD CODES

$a - Replacement heading
Heading that replaces a deleted heading. Each replacement heading is contained in a separate subfield $a.
$i - Explanatory text
Text may be broken up by subfield $a data and may be only a connector term, such as and.

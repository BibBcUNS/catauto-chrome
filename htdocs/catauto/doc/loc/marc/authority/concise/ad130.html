FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Uniform title used in a title or extended subject heading in established heading records or an unestablished uniform title used in a traced or untraced reference record.

In an established heading record, field 130 contains the established form of a uniform title. In a reference record, this field contains an unestablished form of a uniform title.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 130 and input conventions are given in the X30 Uniform Titles-General Information section.
